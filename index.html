Your Photography System Implementation Checklist

Discuss and answer each question below, focusing on areas where decisions still need to be made or protocols finalized. Take notes for follow-up and assign responsibilities.

Documenting Patient Consent

1. Have we created a customized logo-branded photo release form for our practice?

2. Is a printed or fillable PDF of the photo release form included in the new patient documents?

Scheduling

1. Have we decided when clinical photos will be taken of our patients?

Storing & Organizing Images

1. Has an office laptop or tablet been designated for storing and editing patient images?

Marketing

1. Will we use before-and-after patient photos to showcase our clinical skills?

Patient Education

1. Have we started showing patients their clinical images alongside educational videos?
